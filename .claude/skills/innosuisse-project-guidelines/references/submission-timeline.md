## Evaluation Process

### Timeline: 6-8 Weeks

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 0 | Application submitted via Innolink |
| 1-2 | Formal check by Innosuisse Secretariat |
| 2-5 | Expert evaluation (2 independent reviewers) |
| 6 | Innovation Council meeting |
| 6-8 | Decision notification |

### Possible Outcomes

| Decision | Meaning | Next Steps |
|----------|---------|------------|
| **Approved** | Project funded | Contract negotiation, project start |
| **Approved with conditions** | Minor revisions needed | Address conditions, resubmit elements |
| **Rejected** | Not funded | May reapply with improved proposal |

## Pre-Submission Checklist

### 8 Weeks Before Deadline
- [ ] Project concept finalized with partner
- [ ] Team composition confirmed
- [ ] Budget draft prepared
- [ ] Internal approval obtained

### 6 Weeks Before Deadline
- [ ] Application draft complete in Innolink
- [ ] Budget reviewed by grants office
- [ ] Partner signatures obtained
- [ ] Internal review completed

### 4 Weeks Before Deadline
- [ ] Final revisions based on internal feedback
- [ ] All attachments prepared (CVs, financials)
- [ ] Final partner confirmation

### 2 Weeks Before Deadline
- [ ] Final proofread
- [ ] All sections complete in Innolink
- [ ] Test submission (check for errors)

### Submission Day
- [ ] Final check of all sections
- [ ] Confirm partner access/approval
- [ ] Submit before 17:00 Swiss time
- [ ] Save confirmation receipt

## Tips for Timing

### Allow Buffer Time
- Internal reviews take 1-2 weeks
- Partner coordination takes time
- Technical issues with Innolink can occur
- **Recommend: Submit 7 weeks before meeting**

### Coordinate with Partner
- Implementation partner needs time for:
  - Financial documentation (SMEs)
  - Internal approvals
  - Team commitment confirmations
- Start partner coordination 10-12 weeks before deadline

### Institution Requirements
- Research institutions often have internal deadlines
- May require:
  - Department approval
  - Grants office review
  - Legal review of partnership
- Check your institution's internal timeline

## After Submission

### What to Expect

| Timeline | Event |
|----------|-------|
| Within 1 week | Acknowledgment of receipt |
| 2-3 weeks | Possible clarification questions |
| 4-6 weeks | Possible invitation for interview (rare) |
| 6-8 weeks | Decision notification |

### If Asked for Clarifications
- Respond within requested timeframe (usually 1 week)
- Be concise and specific
- Don't introduce new information beyond what's asked

### If Rejected
- Request feedback from Innosuisse
- Address specific weaknesses
- May resubmit improved proposal
- No limit on resubmissions (but address feedback)
